Our Story

A Legacy of Mountain Hospitality

Inspired by Nature

Founded in 2010, Hotel Mount Kailash was born from a deep love for the pristine beauty of Sangla Valley. Our founders, longtime visitors to this magical region, envisioned a retreat that would allow guests to experience the majesty of the Himalayas without compromising on comfort and luxury.

Named after the sacred Mount Kailash, our hotel embodies the spiritual tranquility and awe-inspiring grandeur of the mountains. Every aspect of our design and service philosophy is guided by respect for the natural environment and local traditions.

Over the years, we have grown from a small mountain lodge to a premier destination, while maintaining our commitment to authenticity, sustainability, and exceptional hospitality.

Our Location

Discover Sangla Valley

Nestled in the stunning Sangla Valley at an elevation of 2,700 meters, Hotel Mount Kailash offers you a perfect base to explore the pristine beauty of Kinnaur district in Himachal Pradesh.

The valley, carved by the Baspa River, is surrounded by snow-capped mountains, pine forests, and traditional Kinnauri villages. The region is known for its apple orchards, ancient temples, and rich cultural heritage.

Our hotel is strategically located to provide easy access to popular attractions including:

  • Kamru Fort
  • Bering Nag Temple
  • Chitkul Village
  • Baspa River for trout fishing
  • Trekking trails to Lamkhaga Pass
